In case where the Teacher is sick or cannot take his/her class or ha left college, you can assign an alternate teacher to take his/her place using this page.
- Replace: assign new Teacher to existing teacher. (Existing Teacher is no longer teaching the class)
- Relie: Assign new Teacher as na alternate teacher for the existing class. (Existing Teacher is still responsible for the class)
